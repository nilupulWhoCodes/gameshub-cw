Assassins Creed

Assassin's Creed is a popular action-adventure video game series developed and published by Ubisoft. The series has been ongoing since 2007 and has seen multiple sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations over the years.

The games typically follow a protagonist who is an assassin fighting against a Templar Order or similar organization, often set in various historical periods and locations, such as ancient Egypt, medieval Europe, and the American Revolution. Players are able to explore large open worlds, engage in stealth-based combat, and complete various missions and objectives.

The Assassin's Creed series is known for its blend of historical fiction and science fiction, as well as its focus on parkour-style movement and free-running mechanics. The series has also been adapted into a movie, comics, and novels.

Far Cry

PUBG

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole. The game was first released for Microsoft Windows in 2017, and has since been ported to various other platforms, including Xbox One, PlayStation 4, and mobile devices.

In PUBG, up to 100 players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to kill other players while avoiding being killed themselves. The playable area of the island gradually shrinks over time, forcing players to move closer together and increasing the likelihood of encounters. The last player or team standing wins the game.

PUBG is known for its intense gameplay, realistic graphics, and tactical gameplay. The game features a variety of weapons and vehicles that players can use to gain an advantage over their opponents. It also includes a variety of maps with different terrain and environmental features, which can affect gameplay strategies.

PUBG has become a cultural phenomenon, with millions of players around the world competing in online matches and various esports tournaments. It has also inspired other popular battle royale games, such as Fortnite and Apex Legends.
