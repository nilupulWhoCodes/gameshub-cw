The Game Awards, since its inception, has been one of if not the biggest gaming award show out there. Sure, there are others, but The Game Awards always seems to bring the most hype and excitement. Especially when it comes to their Game of the Year category. Of course, when choosing the best anything, opinions will clash. With that in mind, let’s look back at the previous Game of the Year winners, and you can decide if The Game Awards got it right or wrong.

CREATOR


GEOFF KEIGHLEY



THE GAME AWARDS WAS FOUNDED IN 2014 BY MEDIA ENTREPRENEUR GEOFF KEIGHLEY, WHO ALSO HOSTS AND PRODUCES THE PROGRAM.

Over the past two decades, Keighley has served as a journalist, host, and producer of video game programming across multiple platforms, including print, television and digital.

Keighley is best known for his work with Entertainment Weekly, Spike TV, G4, and YouTube, as well as his landmark series of behind the scenes game development stories known as “The Final Hours.” Geoff also currently produces gamescom's “Opening Night Live” program.

Elden Ring is an upcoming action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware, the creators of Dark Souls and Bloodborne, in collaboration with George R. R. Martin, the author of the A Song of Ice and Fire series. The game takes place in a world created by Martin and features gameplay mechanics similar to those found in FromSoftware's previous games. Players will explore a vast open world, encounter various enemies, and engage in challenging combat while uncovering the game's lore and story. The game has been highly anticipated by fans of both FromSoftware and Martin's work, and it is expected to offer a unique and immersive gaming experience.

CREATOR


HIDETAKA MIZEYAKI



Hidetaka Miyazaki is a video game designer and director, known for his work on several critically acclaimed titles, including Dark Souls and Bloodborne. Miyazaki is the president of FromSoftware and is considered one of the most influential figures in the gaming industry.

He is known for his unique vision, challenging gameplay mechanics, and his ability to create immersive and atmospheric worlds.

In collaboration with George R.R. Martin, Miyazaki is the lead creator and director of Elden Ring.His involvement in the development of Elden Ring has generated a lot of excitement among fans of FromSoftware, and many are eagerly anticipating the release of the game.

It Takes Two is a cooperative action-adventure game developed by Hazelight Studios and published by Electronic Arts. Players control a couple turned into dolls, Cody and May, who must work together to navigate through challenges in a fantastical world to break the spell and regain their human forms. With a focus on cooperative gameplay and a variety of environments and mechanics, the game has been praised for its unique approach to co-op and heartfelt story, making it a popular choice among gamers.

CREATOR


JOESF FARES



Lebanese-Swedish video game designer and director who founded Hazelight Studios in 2014.

Fares is known for his innovative approach to game design and storytelling, as well as his emphasis on cooperative play. His previous game, A Way Out, was also a cooperative-focused title that received critical acclaim for its unique gameplay mechanics and emotional storytelling.

With It Takes Two, Fares has once again delivered a game that blends compelling gameplay with a heartfelt narrative, cementing his status as one of the most exciting game creators working today.

The Last of Us Part II is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog, released in 2020. The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world and follows the story of Ellie, a young woman who embarks on a mission of revenge after a tragic event. The game features gameplay mechanics similar to its predecessor, with a focus on stealth and survival elements, as well as intense combat sequences. The Last of Us Part II has been praised for its compelling storytelling, complex characters, and stunning visuals, as well as its mature and nuanced exploration of themes such as loss, trauma, and the cycle of violence. The game has won numerous awards and is considered one of the best games of its generation.

CREATOR


NEIL DRUCKMANN



Israeli-American writer, creative director, designer, and programmer who has been co-president of the video game developer Naughty Dog since 2020.

Druckmann has a strong background in game development and has worked on several successful titles, including the first Last of Us game, Uncharted 4: A Thief's End, and Uncharted: The Lost Legacy.

His creative vision for The Last of Us Part II helped to make it one of the most highly anticipated games of 2020, with the game's strong narrative and gameplay mechanics receiving widespread critical acclaim.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure game developed by FromSoftware, set in a fictionalized version of Japan's Sengoku period. Players control a shinobi named Sekiro, tasked with rescuing his young master from a samurai clan. The game's intense combat relies heavily on timing and precision, featuring a unique "posture" system. Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice received widespread critical acclaim for its challenging gameplay, immersive storytelling, and stunning visuals, becoming one of FromSoftware's most popular titles and winning Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2019.

CREATOR


HIDETAKA MIZEYAKI



Hidetaka Miyazaki is a video game designer and director, known for his work on several critically acclaimed titles, including Dark Souls and Bloodborne. Miyazaki is the president of FromSoftware and is considered one of the most influential figures in the gaming industry.

He is known for his unique vision, challenging gameplay mechanics, and his ability to create immersive and atmospheric worlds.

Miyazaki's involvement in Sekiro was significant, as he oversaw the game's development and design. Sekiro features many of the gameplay mechanics that Miyazaki is known for, including challenging combat, a deep and immersive world, and intricate character design. The game was critically acclaimed upon release, receiving praise for its unique take on the action-adventure genre and its captivating story. Miyazaki's involvement in Sekiro was a major contributing factor to the game's success and has solidified his reputation as one of the industry's most influential game designers.

God of War is a popular video game franchise that follows the story of Kratos, a Spartan warrior who seeks revenge against the gods of Olympus. The series is known for its fast-paced, action-packed gameplay, impressive graphics, and epic storytelling. The original game was released in 2005 for the PlayStation 2, and since then, several sequels and spin-offs have been released on various platforms. In the most recent installment of the series, also titled God of War, Kratos is older and wiser, having moved on from his past and now raising his son in a world inspired by Norse mythology. The game was released in 2018 for the PlayStation 4 and received critical acclaim for its immersive world-building, emotional storytelling, and innovative gameplay mechanics.

CREATOR


DAVID JAFFE



Game designer and director at Sony Computer Entertainment's Santa Monica Studio.

Jaffe initially conceived the game as an adaptation of the Greek myth of Theseus, but the project later evolved into the story of Kratos, a Spartan warrior seeking revenge against the gods of Olympus.

Jaffe directed the first two games in the series and served as a creative consultant on subsequent titles. He is also known for his work on other popular games, such as Twisted Metal and Calling All Cars.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an action-adventure game developed by Nintendo for their Switch console and Wii U. It features an open-world environment where players can explore the vast world of Hyrule. The game has a dynamic weather system, crafting, and survival mechanics, an expansive story and side quests. Players use various weapons and abilities to solve puzzles, defeat enemies, and traverse the game's terrain. Breath of the Wild has won several awards, including the Game of the Year award at The Game Awards 2017. It is considered a masterpiece of modern gaming and a must-play for fans of the Zelda series and open-world games.

CREATOR


EIJI AONUMA



Veteran game designer and producer at Nintendo. He was the director of Breath of the Wild and oversaw the game's overall design and development.

Under Aonuma's leadership, Breath of the Wild marked a significant departure from the traditional Zelda formula, with its open-world environment and gameplay mechanics. He was instrumental in shaping the game's vast and immersive world, including its dynamic weather system, crafting, and survival mechanics. Aonuma's vision and leadership helped create a game that has been widely praised for its innovation and design.

Aonuma's involvement in the Zelda franchise has spanned more than two decades, and his contributions have played a significant role in the series' success. His work on Breath of the Wild has cemented his place as one of the most influential game designers in the industry, and his legacy will continue to shape the future of the Zelda series and gaming as a whole.

Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter developed by Blizzard Entertainment. Players work together using a diverse cast of heroes with unique abilities and playstyles to complete objectives and defeat the enemy team across a variety of modes and maps. The game is known for its engaging gameplay, emphasis on teamwork and strategy, and vibrant art style. Overwatch has a large and active community, with ongoing updates and new heroes and maps being released by Blizzard to keep the game fresh and exciting for players.

CREATOR


JEFF KAPLAN



The game director for Overwatch from its inception until he left the company in 2021.

Kaplan was a key figure in the development of Overwatch, providing creative direction and overseeing the game's design and gameplay mechanics. He was known for his strong commitment to the game's community and his willingness to engage with players and take their feedback into account when making changes to the game.

Kaplan's involvement in the development of Overwatch helped to make it one of the most popular and successful multiplayer games of all time. His leadership and vision were instrumental in shaping the game into the beloved title it is today, and his departure from Blizzard was a significant loss for the company and the Overwatch community.

TThe Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red, based on the book series by Andrzej Sapkowski. Players control Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, as he navigates a vast open-world filled with diverse environments and engaging in various activities such as crafting and playing mini-games. The game's branching storyline with multiple endings, stunning graphics, and immersive world has made it a beloved classic and winner of numerous awards.

CREATOR


MARCIN IWINSKI



Founder of CD Projekt Red, a Polish video game company

Iwiński was actively involved in the development of The Witcher 3, serving as a producer and overseeing many aspects of the game's creation. His commitment to quality and attention to detail are evident in the game's stunning graphics, rich storytelling, and engaging gameplay mechanics.

The success of The Witcher 3 has cemented CD Projekt Red's reputation as a leading developer of high-quality video games and has made Iwiński and his team highly respected figures in the gaming industry.

Dragon Age: Inquisition is an action role-playing game developed by BioWare and published by Electronic Arts. Players take on the role of the Inquisitor, tasked with saving the world from a mysterious force known as the Breach. Featuring an open-world environment, players can explore various locations, interact with different characters, and engage in combat with enemies. With a deep and immersive storyline, player choices affecting the outcome of the game, and the ability to customize characters and make moral decisions that impact the game's world, Dragon Age: Inquisition is considered one of the best RPGs of recent years.

CREATOR


MIKE LAIDLAW



The lead designer of the game.

Laidlaw's involvement in the development of Inquisition was significant, as he played a key role in shaping the game's story and gameplay mechanics. Under his direction, the game introduced several new features, including an open-world environment, improved combat mechanics, and a more complex and dynamic story that offered players meaningful choices that impacted the game's outcome.

Laidlaw's contribution to the game's success was widely recognized, and Dragon Age: Inquisition went on to win several awards, including Game of the Year at The Game Awards in 2014.
